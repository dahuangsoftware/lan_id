Yuexi County, Sichuan is a county of Sichuan Province, China. It is under the administration of the Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture.

== Fauna ==
The frog species Oreolalax liangbeiensis, Oreolalax puxiongensis, and Oreolalax pingii are endemic to Yuexi County. The former two have only been recorded in Puxiong (普雄镇).
