Michele Buck is an American businesswoman. In March 2017 she became the President and CEO of The Hershey Company, an American food manufacturing company, replacing former CEO John Bilbrey.She serves on the Board of Directors at New York Life, and as a Benefit Co-Chair for the Children’s Brain Tumor Foundation. A native of central Pennsylvania, Buck earned a Bachelor’s degree at Shippensburg University of Pennsylvania and holds a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
