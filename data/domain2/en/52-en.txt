TVS (Telewizja Silesia, Television Schlesien, Telewizja Śląska) is a first regional television channel profile infotainment. TVS channel is directed primarily to residents of the Silesian Voivodeship, including the historic lands of Upper Silesia and Lesser Poland (including Dąbrowa Basin and Kraków Basin). TVS channel was created as competition for TVP Katowice.
TVS channel it started broadcasting on March 29, 2008 at 10:00 am. According to market share in December 2012 number is 0,45%.(December 2012)
