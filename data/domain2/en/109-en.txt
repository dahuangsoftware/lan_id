The 1975 Hawaii Open, also known by its sponsored name Island Holidays Pro Classic, was a men's tennis tournament played an outdoor hard courts in Maui, Hawaii, USA. It was the second edition of the tournament and was held from 29 September through 5 October 1975. The tournament was part of the Grand Prix tennis circuit and categorized in Group B. First-seeded Jimmy Connors won the singles title.


== Winners ==


=== Men's Singles ===

 Jimmy Connors defeated  Sandy Mayer 6–1, 6–0


=== Men's Doubles ===

 Fred McNair /  Sherwood Stewart defeated  Jeff Borowiak /  Haroon Rahim 3–6, 7–6, 6–3
