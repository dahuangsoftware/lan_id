Edward Ajado (1 March 1929 – 26 December 1980) is a Nigerian sprinter. He competed in the 100 metres at the 1952 Summer Olympics and the 1956 Summer Olympics. He placed second in the 1954 British Empire and Commonwealth Games 4×110 yards Relay (with Abdul Karim Amu, Karim Olowu, and Muslim Arogundade). In the 1954 British Empire and Commonwealth Games 100 yards Ajado finished fourth.
