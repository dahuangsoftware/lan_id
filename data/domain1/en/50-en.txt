Chah-e Dasht Mohammad Khan 1 (Persian: چاه دشت محمدخان 1‎‎, also Romanized as Chāh-e Dasht Moḩammad Khān 1; also known as Fāṭemīyeh) is a village in Sahra Rural District, Anabad District, Bardaskan County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 43, in 13 families.
