Hôpital Paul-Brousse is a hospital in Villejuif, South Paris, France. It is named after Paul Brousse, a French socialist.
Marc Zelter MD and Daniel Vittecoq MD are the professors in this hospital.
Camille Loiseau, the Doyenne de France from March 26, 2005 to August 12, 2006, died at the Hôpital Paul-Brousse.
