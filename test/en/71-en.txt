Jefferson Academy Charter School or JA is a K-12 charter school in unincorporated Jefferson County, Colorado, United States. The K-6 campus is located at 9955 Yarrow St, Broomfield, CO 80021 and the 7-12 campus is located at 11251 Reed Way Broomfield, CO 80020.


== History ==
Jefferson Academy was founded in 1994.
In February 2013, Jefferson Academy opened a brand new $9.6 million facility for the junior high and high school students.
