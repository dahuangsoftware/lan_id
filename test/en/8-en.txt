J. Merchant a.k.a. J. Marchant (first name and dates unknown) was an English professional cricketer who made 5 known appearances in first-class cricket matches from 1791 to 1792.


== Career ==
He was mainly associated with Sussex (a.k.a. Brighton). His surname was spelt Merchant in some scorecards and Marchant in others.
