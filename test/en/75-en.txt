25CN-NBOMe (2C-CN-NBOMe, NBOMe-2C-CN) is a derivative of the phenethylamine 2C-CN. It acts in a similar manner to related compounds such as 25I-NBOMe, which are potent agonists at the 5HT2A receptor.
