Jegielnica [jɛɡʲɛlˈnit͡sa] (German: Juglitz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Korfantów, within Nysa County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) south-west of Korfantów, 15 km (9 mi) east of Nysa, and 38 km (24 mi) south-west of the regional capital Opole.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see Territorial changes of Poland after World War II).
The village has a population of 185.
